FREDDY

Character setting:

A typical stage protagonist, a friendly image that symbolizes a mascot, but his essence is extremely dangerous after being "possessed".

Story:

Freddy Fazbear is the central antagonist and mascot of Five Nights at Freddy's. He is an animatronic bear. Along with the other animatronics, he wanders the pizzeria until 6 AM. Freddy has appeared in various forms throughout the series.
